Web development / design meetup

November 10, 2012 @ Uusi Tehdas, Tampere

About

The second bi-annual Tampere Web geek meetup is just around the corner. Join us for a nice day of presentations, networking and possibly a beer or two!

The event is hosted in co-operation with the awesome guys & gals at Devaamo and New Factory, and sponsored by Leonidas and Futurice.

You can check the location in Google Maps.
