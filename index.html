the tampere web development /
design meetup

The second bi-annual Tampere Web geek meetup is just around the corner. Join us for a nice day of presentations, networking and possibly a beer or two!

The event is hosted in co-operation with the awesome guys & gals at Devaamo and New Factory, and sponsored by Leonidas and Futurice.

Get your (free) ticket at http://treweb.eventbrite.com before it's too late!

Talks

Here's the fantastic folks who have offered to present their experiences. Would you like to as well? If so, please register your talk: http://treweb.wufoo.com/forms/treweb-12-2-speakers/

  • Jarkko Laine: Scaling your frontend – graphics in the era of retina screens    
  • Olli Mahlamäki: jQuery Mobile - the Good, the Bad and the Ugly    
  • Antti Järvinen: Enterprise Javascript with XMLVM    
  • Jouni Kaplas: Windows 8 + JavaScript. Does it really suck?    
  • Timo Lyytinen: Building single page web applications with MarionetteJS    
  • Jaakko Salonen: Silver Bullet Cloud Unicorn Stories - Or: Lessons Learned from Using Node.js in Production    

The exact schedule is yet to be determined. Watch this space for updates.
